📖 Admin Guide — how to use this dashboard

Tap any topic to expand. New here? Start with How it works.

How it works
The big picture in 30 seconds
›
Drivers send document photos in a Telegram group → the bot bundles them into a Pending review → you check the photos, run a quick checklist, assign a company & truck, and approve. Approved drivers are organized by company and can be auto-posted to a Telegram channel.
  1. Driver sends photos in a group the bot is in.
  2. Bot collects them and creates a review in Pending Reviews.
  3. You review & approve (or reject).
  4. Approved driver appears under Drivers and (optionally) posts to your channel.
Reviewing & approving
Pending Reviews tab
›
  • Click any row to open the review window.
  • Click photos to select which ones to approve (✓ = selected).
  • Use the verification checklist to flag issues (no manuals / no tablet / no paper log).
  • Optionally set a Company and Truck number.
  • Press Approve Driver, or Reject.
Quick actions: the green ✓ in the list approves all photos instantly; the red ✕ rejects — no need to open the row.
The two-name system
Drivers & Groups
›
Each driver and group shows two names:
  • The big name is your admin label — a clear name only you set (e.g. John — Volvo, MD-1204).
  • The small grey name tagged TG is the original Telegram name, kept for reference and never overwritten.
To add a label: open a driver → Edit Driver, or a group → Rename. Rows marked no label haven't been labeled yet. Search matches both names.
Companies
Organize drivers
›
  • Add a company with the form at the top of the Companies tab.
  • Assign drivers during approval, or via Edit Driver.
  • Click a company to see its drivers; edit or delete from there.
A company with assigned drivers can't be deleted — reassign those drivers first.
Telegram groups & sync
Groups tab
›
  • Add the bot to your group. It registers automatically when added or when it sees activity.
  • Press Sync Groups to refresh names, member counts & active status.
  • A group is marked inactive only when the bot is actually removed — a temporary network error won't drop it.
  • Rename a group (admin label), Send Message to one group, or Broadcast to all active groups.
Important: in @BotFather → /setprivacy → Disable, so the bot can see all group messages (needed to auto-detect drivers' photos).
Daily scheduled messages
Auto-send every day
›
In the Groups tab, open Daily scheduled message:
  1. Type your message (Markdown supported).
  2. Pick the time it should go out each day.
  3. Choose All active groups or tick specific groups.
  4. Press Schedule daily message.
The bot resends it every day at that time until you delete it. Use ⏸ to pause and ▶ to resume. Times use the server's local clock.
Channel auto-posting
Publish approved drivers
›
  • Set your Channel ID below (the bot must be an admin of the channel).
  • When you approve a driver, their info + approved photos post to the channel automatically.
  • Use Send Drivers List to post a summary of all approved drivers on demand.
Find a channel ID by forwarding any channel post to @userinfobot. Channel IDs look like -1001234567890.
Shortcuts & tips
Work faster
›
  • Press / to jump to the search box on any tab.
  • Press Esc to collapse expanded rows.
  • The <1> <2> numbers help you reference rows quickly.
  • Long lists paginate at 20 items per page.
🚀 First-time setup checklist
1
Disable bot privacy

In @BotFather → /setprivacy → Disable, so the bot sees group photos.

2
Add the bot to your driver groups

Then open the Groups tab and press Sync Groups.

3
Create your companies

In the Companies tab, so you can assign drivers on approval.

4
Set your channel (optional)

Add the bot as a channel admin and save the Channel ID below.

5
You're ready

Drivers send photos → review them in Pending Reviews → approve.

Approved drivers are automatically posted here. The bot must be an admin of the channel.
